inno.space does not work alone! We are happy to be integrated into three different networks, allowing us to collaborate locally and internationally, exchange knowledge and grow alongside over 30 partners worldwide.

Sugar

The SUGAR Network is a global platform of universities focused on creating and delivering a new learning experience by applying Human Centered Design to solve real world problems.
The network counts on 24 universities spread around the globe across 4 continents and institutions such as Aalto University, Kyoto Institute of Technology, Swinburne University of Technology and the Trinity College Dublin.

DFGN

Design Factory Global Network (DFGN) is a network of innovation hubs in universities and research organisations in five continents of the world. A Design Factory is, essentialy, a platform for co-creation between students, universities and organizations through passion-based learning and collaboration among different cultures. If you have the chance of visiting more Design Factories, you will see how we all share the same values, ideals and how similar we all look!
